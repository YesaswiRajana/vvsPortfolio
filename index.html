About Me
Veligatla Venkata Suresh, student at GITAM,pursuing my graduation in the stream of Bachelor of Architecture. I love to create interactive sketches,plans,3d Models.Apart from this I love to explore new places and capture moments in a creative way.
I am a young enthusiast fueled by the fusion of two worlds: art and photography . With a camera in hand, I embark on journeys, capturing the beauty of the world around me with each click of the shutter. From the bustling streets of the city to the serene landscapes of nature, My eyes find inspiration everywhere I looks. For me the thrill lies in the exploration and the endless possibilities that both photography and architecture , and I eagerly embraces every opportunity to hone my skills and expand horizons.
